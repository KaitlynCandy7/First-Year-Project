C-Class is World Car of the Year.

Christoph Horn, Head of Global Communication Passenger Cars Mercedes-Benz and Rob Moran, Director Mercedes-Benz USA Communications, received the awards on behalf of the brand with the three pointed star at a ceremony held on the occasion of the New York International Auto Show. Particular success was enjoyed by the Mercedes-Benz C-Class, which won the World Car of the Year 2015 Award.

The S-Class Coupe was voted World Luxury Car 2015, while the Mercedes-AMG GT sports car picked up the award for World Performance Car 2015. Mercedes-Benz was the only automobile manufacturer to make it into the final round of the awards with five vehicles.
