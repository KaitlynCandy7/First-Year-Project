A 1954 Mercedes-Benz race car sold for a record $30 million at an auction in England Friday.

This particular Mercedes Benz makes it to the top of this list because it represents priceless history. This car is the same car that was used by famous formula 1 race car driver, Juan Manuel Fangio, to set a new formula 1 world record. This is precisely why this car costs 29.6 million dollars. You might think this is an unbelievable price to pay for a car that is not so powerful in todays standard, however, its old school appearance is part of the reason behind its alluring history.
